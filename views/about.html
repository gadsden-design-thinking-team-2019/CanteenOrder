Canteen Orders


Carey Baptist Grammar School

This website allows students to pre-order and pay for their lunch and recess meals/snacks to skip the canteen line. Students order their food online and only the canteen staff can view studentâ€™s orders. The way it works is students order their food online before school or roll call then the order gets put through to the staff ready to collect at lunch/recess. This website reduces the line for the canteen and skips the dilemma of what to get when students are actually in the canteen.

Payment is similar to the current print credit system. Parents can use credit/debit cards to top up the students order and the guardian/parent can be notified if activated when a student orders something.
